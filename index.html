Bowen's Coastal Paradise Awaits

A tropical paradise like no other

Welcome to the picturesque coastal gem of Queensland, Australia, a tranquil haven nestled between the crystal-clear waters of the Coral Sea and lush tropical landscapes. This small coastal town, with its laid-back charm and friendly locals, invites you to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and embrace the serenity of seaside living.

You're going to fall in love with Bowen

The town's vibrant esplanade is lined with quaint shops, seafood restaurants, and charming cafes, providing the perfect backdrop for leisurely strolls as the sun sets over the horizon.

Explore the reef

Bowen is surrounded by beautiful beaches and vibrant reefs, making it a world class destination for snorkeling and diving.

Take a hike

Walk along one of the many trails along coastline, beaches, hills, and bushland araound Bowen. Keep your eyes open for wildlife!

Post card views

Visit some of the lookouts around town for stunning nature views. Perfect locations to snap those special vacation pictures.

Play a round

Bowen Gold Club, a beautiful 18 hole golf course right next to the beach. Afterwards, head into the club house for a cold drink and a meal.
